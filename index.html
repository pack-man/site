What is Packman?

Packman is a platform that automates software packaging creation and management.
Our platform relieves engineering teams from the need to allocate a lot of resources to package creation and maintenance for their software.

The Packman revolution allows engineering teams to simply define their package specifications. Our platform will then create the right packages for all the target platforms, run acceptance tests, and distribute the packages to the appropriate repository.

Packman is a free and open source software, which will utilize a SaaS revenue model where users will be able to leverage professional services, storage, delivery, and other services for a fee.

Why do you need Packman

Packman provides the ability to smoothly automate package lifecycle activities (creation, maintenance, updates, tests, etc.) without the need to hire packaging engineers or pay for consulting services.
Therefore companies can use Packman to increase their installation success rates, usage, and end user satisfaction. Engineering teams can focus on advancing their products instead of the menial task of creating and optimizing software packages.
